1. AGE REQUIREMENTS FOR RECREATIONAL ENTRY TICKETS

Provisions for children who have already been charged an entrance ticket:

- Ancol: children aged 2 years and over are charged an entrance ticket to Ancol

- Dufan: children with a height of 100 cm and above are subject to a Dufan entrance ticket

Especially for elderly visitors who want to visit a fantasy world dufan, they get a 50% discount, with the following conditions:

- Especially for elderly visitors with a minimum age of 60 years

- Only valid for regular tickets, according to the prices that apply

at the Dufan offline counter (not valid for online tickets)

- It is mandatory to show ID when purchasing tickets

at the Dufan offline counter

- The cashier must record the ID number of elderly visitors when making transactions

- 1 (one) senior ID card is only valid for purchasing 1 (one) ticket

2. TICKET REFUND?

In accordance with the terms and conditions stated at the time of ticket purchase that the ticket is non-refundable.

3. RESCHEDULE TICKET

In accordance with the provisions stated at the time of purchasing the ticket, that the ticket can only be used according to the selected date. Rescheduling of visits is only allowed for visitors who have purchased tickets at dufan.com but cannot enter Dufan because they fall into the following categories:

- Body temperature above 37.3 degrees Celsius

- Are in a state of illness that leads to symptoms of covid-19

4. PROVISIONS FOR VISITORS WHO MAY ENTER DUFAN ?

- Age 18 and over must have a follow-up dose (booster) vaccine or green status on the care-protect application

- Age 6 - 17 years old has 2 doses of vaccine, ages under 6 years old can make recreational visits provided that a parental companion has received an advanced dose of vaccine (booster)

5. DUFAN AND ANCOL OPERATING HOURS

To enjoy the beauty of each Ancol beach area, visitors must pay attention to the operating hours that apply:

DUFAN

- Weekdays (Monday - Friday)

*Fantasy world: 10.00 a.m. - 05.00 p.m.

*Ancol gate: 09.00 a.m. -16.00 p.m.

- Weekend (Saturday, Sunday and national holidays)

*Fantasy world: 10.00 a.m. - 19.00 p.m.

*Ancol gate: 09.00 a.m. - 06.00 p.m.

ANCOL

- Weekdays (Monday - Friday)

*Ancol gate: 06.00 a.m. to 08.00 p.m.

*Park and beach: 06.00 a.m. to 09.00 p.m.

- Weekend (Saturday, Sunday and national holidays)

*Ancol gate: 06.00 a.m. to 09.00 p.m.

*Park and beach: 06.00 a.m. to 10.00 p.m.
