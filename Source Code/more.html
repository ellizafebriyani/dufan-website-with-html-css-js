WHAT IS "DUFAN ANCOL"??

Dunia Fantasi or more popularly known as Dufan, first opened to the public on August 29, 1985 and is the first theme park developed by the Company and already has ISO 9001:2015 certificate since February 2017. Apart from being an entertainment center outdoor, Dufan is also the largest physics edutainment area in Indonesia, which pampers visitors with Fantasy Around the World, through high-tech game rides, which are divided into 9 (nine) regions namely Indonesia, Jakarta, Asia, Europe, America, Greece, Hikayat, Kalila, and Fantasy Lights.


Fasilities

Various public facilities for Dufan visitors, namely Free wifi in every area, charger points in every area, clean toilets in every area and rides, baby care rooms, prayer rooms, mosques, first aid and ambulances.


History of Dufan and Monkeys

  The Dufan project was launched in the early 1980s when at that time Ancol was still under the leadership of Mr. Handogo Soekarno who had as head of the promotion division of Taman Impian Jaya Ancol. At that time, Handogo gave a mandate to Benny Benhardi to make illustrations using Birds Eye View. This work was carried out at the Balai Samudra Architecture office under the direction of Mr. Sutisna and senior architect Budi Priambodo and several draftsmen. Dunia Fantasi has several mascots, one of which is a proboscis monkey named Dufan (short for Dunia Fantasi).


  The choice of ape as a character is to remind us that Ancol used to be a monkey area. Meanwhile, the selection of the proboscis monkey is solely to introduce endangered species that are now protected. At first, the cartoon form of the proboscis monkey was visualized by Matari Advertising who participated in the initial Dunia Fantasi communication program because at that time, Agustinus Teddy Darmanto, as chairman and person in charge of BenDufa (World Fantasy Workshop) was very busy in the final completion stage. Therefore, the visualization is given to Matari Advertising. However, after Dunia Fantasi opened to the public, BenDufa's team took over the visualization of Dufan's character and created the logo and the characters of Dunia Fantasi.



Fantasy World Area (Dufan)


  Dufan is divided into several areas with their own themes and characteristics of each region. The distribution of this area is intended to awaken the imagination of visitors who are expected to be able to feel the sensation of Fantasy Around the World and Dufan's Modern Era starting from Jakarta, America, Doll Palace, Indonesia, Europe, Asia, Saga, Adventure, Greece, Indoor Dufan, and Cartoon World.



  Apart from rides and shows, Dufan also has a number of restaurants, merchandise shops, and arcade games. Dunia Fantasi's area reaches 21 hectares of the planned development of the 552 hectares integrated entertainment area Taman Impian Jaya Ancol. Ancol offers outdoor amusement rides but also provides rides for games with sophisticated technology to fantasize around the world which consists of 9 areas starting from Indonesia, Jakarta, Europe, Asia, Greece, America, Hikayat, Fantasy Light and Kalila.



  As a vehicle for fantasy as the largest physics tourism area in Indonesia, Dunia Fantasi (Dufan), Ancol has added another new area, namely Indoor Dufan. During 2014-2015 it has been equipped with Kontiki, Ice Age, and Hello Kitty rides. There are various public facilities in Dufan Ancol that can be enjoyed by all visitors, including prayer rooms and mosques, first aid, ambulances, charger points, baby care rooms, clean and safe toilets, free wifi, free drinking water, free loans for babies stollers and wheelchairs.


  Visitors can come and visit Dufan on weekdays from 10.00 a.m. to 06.00 p.m. and on weekends from 10.00 a.m. to 08.00 p.m. Meanwhile, to get to the Dufan Ancol area, you can use a variety of transportation options which include:

1. Train to Ancol: with the Tangerang route, to Serpong, to Depok, Bekasi and Bogor.

2. Trans Jakarta Bus: with the Ancol route to Kampung Melayu, Kampung Rambutan or Ancol to Lebak Bulus, Pulo Gadung, Ragunan, Blok M, or Ancol to Harmoni directly (Corridor 5)

3. Private vehicles to Ancol can be accessed via the toll road. In addition to some of these transportations, tourists can use microbuses or special transportation in Ancol, Transportation in Taman Impian Jaya Ancol (Wira-Wiri Bus, Bicycle, Segway, Golf Cart, Gondola)

DUFAN THEME PARK

Ontang Anting

The Ontang-Anting moves 360 degrees clockwise that makes visitors feel like they are floating in the air. Don't underestimate this games because it will spin constantly causing nausea.

Hysteria

The Hysteria is still a hit in 2023. This ride will make you feel a sensation like thrown into the sky at super high speed. And as the name implies, you will be hysterical and definitely going to scream screaming.

Halilintar

The Halilintar will take visitors across the incline, descents, turning 360 degrees, and corners at high speed. Visitors will feel a surge of adrenaline and tension level which will prompt to scream hysterically.

Leaning House

If in Italy there is a leaning tower, in Jakarta there is what is called a leaning house. This is the only vehicle that does require engine power. Even so, this house is safe and will not collapse! Challenge yourself!

Niagara Gara

Like the name taken from the United States waterfall. This Dufan vehicle will bring its visitors to feel the sensation of jumping from a height. This boat, which can be occupied by more than one person, moves on rails filled with water.

Baku Toki

This vehicle which has another name bomb-bomb car is also the most popular vehicle here, both among children and adolescents. The excitement of crashing a car into other cars cannot be found in other rides. Fasten your seat belt, step on the gas pedal and aim at the target car you want to crash.

Kolibri

Humans cannot fly, but on this Dufan vehicle, visitors can feel the sensation of flying in the air. Hummingbird itself is taken from a bird name and matches its name. This vehicle invites what it's like to be a hummingbird. Don't worry, kids can ride it.

Doll Castle

Don't worry, the dolls here are not dolls with scary faces, but dolls that depict the culture of the Indonesian Archipelago and other countries. Passengers will move on a boat that crosses the river, where there are sophisticated animatronics on the right and left.

Caravel

This vehicle for children invites passengers to feel like floating in the air. This small boat-shaped vehicle with various wavy trajectories will rotate 360Â°. With sets like at sea, children can imagine being sailors who are facing ferocious waves.

Tornado

Passengers are positioned to sit back to back in parallel rows of seats. In one game, able to carry 40 people at once. Put your heart to the test by holding back the adrenaline while flipping through the air.

Happy Feet

Apart from the Samudra Arena, Dufan also has his own theater which is no less interesting. Happy Feet is a 3D theater simulator that suffers from the life of penguins in the North Pole. This vehicle is suitable for educating children while playing.

Turbo Drop

Devoted to lovers of heights. Turbo Drop will drop passengers from a height of 8 meters in an instant and suddenly. In fact, this vehicle will spin the passengers for several times.

Hello Kitty Adventure

This Dufan vehicle is a paradise for Hello Kitty lovers. With a room design that is dominated by pink, it further enhances the adorable feel.Visitors can meet the character of a white cat with ribbons aka Hello Kitty and her friends.

Mystery Train

The Mystery Train or Haunted Coaster takes the setting of a werewolf castle in the 19th century. According to legend, anyone who stepped foot into the castle would die in name only. For those who are looking for a train ride experience that makes the hairs stand on end, be sure to try this sled ride.

Rafting (Arung Jeram)

It's time to play water on this one Dufan vehicle. Don't expect your clothes to stay dry after riding this ride because visitors will feel a sensation like being slammed when a rubber tire runs down an artificial river. Automatically, once in a while the river water will splash and wet the clothes. So, make sure to bring spare clothes before deciding to try this exciting ride.
